About

We provide each child with a fun loving and caring environment where they can learn and grow. The strategy of our primary caregiver Jyothi Deepak who has ~8 years of experience in a variety of teaching techniques at some of the best schools. We bring out the best in children while nurturing them in innovative ways which helps their growth . Motherly affection, home cooked meals, and personalized care allow the children to feel comfortable. Little Millennium has been a Licensed Daycare . 
​Our goal at Little Millennium is to prepare children for the next stage of their lives through the development of motor abilities, thinking skills and healthy interactions with their peers. Keep reading to learn more ! 
​

Our Programs


We have designed and adapted programs to meet the high standards of today’s world of education. By providing a supportive environment, we make sure each child receives exactly what they need.We have a Unique Creative Program for children from 3 Months through 12 years

  • We focus on Language, Gross Motor and Cognitive Development.
  • Interactive learning through Music and Movement.
  • Creative activities and educational games.
  • We have a structured curriculum for daily learning through Montessori methods.

Our Program Includes        

  • Full Day (8 am to 6 pm)              
  • Half Day (8 am to 11:30 am)              
  • Extended Day (8 am to 2:30 pm)
  • After school activities (3 pm to 6 pm)
